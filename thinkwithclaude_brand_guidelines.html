Voice & tone
ThinkWithClaude sounds like a founder who's already done the work. Confident, specific, and free of marketing-speak. Every line should pass the "would a busy exec read this?" test.

✓ Use
"Save 3 hours in your first week or I refund you — no questions asked."
Specific claim, skin in the game, removes risk
✗ Avoid
"Transform your productivity journey with our holistic AI-powered solutions!"
Zero specificity, buzzword-heavy, untrustworthy
✓ Use
"I work on your real clients, your real tools, your real brand."
Repetition creates rhythm and specificity simultaneously
✗ Avoid
"We provide customized AI training solutions for marketing teams."
Corporate "we," no personality, no proof
✓ Use
"50+ trained. 8 years marketing. One intensive."
Short punchy fragments build trust fast
✗ Avoid
"Are you struggling with productivity? You're not alone! Let's go on this journey together! 🚀🔥"
Patronising, emoji-heavy, undermines expertise
Interaction design
Motion principles
Motion should feel like the product is alive — not anxious. Every animation earns its place by communicating state.
→

Forward float — CTA signal

Used on primary CTAs and progress milestones. Signals momentum without demanding attention. ease-in-out · 2.4s · infinite · translateY -7px

Pulse — Live / active state

Used on live session indicators and real-time counts. Mirrors the "●" badge on your live badge. ease-out · 2s · infinite · scale + opacity

Shimmer — Loading skeleton

Replaces spinners. Used while sessions or data is loading. Keeps the UI alive and responsive. linear · 1.6s · infinite · translateX
